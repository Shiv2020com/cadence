Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. What we collect

When you use Cadence, we collect the information you provide directly — your name, email address, and the content of your morning, midday, and evening check-ins. We also collect basic device and usage data (app version, general region, and crash logs) to keep the product working reliably.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate your personal pattern insights and weekly digest
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To improve app stability and fix bugs
  • To send you account-related notices, such as billing receipts

3. What we never do

We do not sell your check-in data or personal information to advertisers, data brokers, employers, or insurers. We do not use your entries to train third-party models without your explicit, separate consent.

4. Data storage and security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is limited to a small number of engineers on a strict need-to-know basis, and all access is logged.

5. Your rights

You can export your full check-in history at any time from Settings → Data. You can also request permanent deletion of your account and associated data, which we complete within 30 days.

6. Third-party services

We use a small number of trusted providers for hosting, analytics, and payment processing. Each is bound by a data processing agreement and only receives the minimum information required to perform its function.

7. Children's privacy

Cadence is intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify active users by email before the changes take effect.

9. Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@cadence.app.
