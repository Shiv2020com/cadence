How it works

Structured around the day you already have.

Cadence doesn't ask you to change your schedule. It moves through three checkpoints that most days already contain.

06:00 – 10:00

Morning

A quick intention-setting check-in. Cadence surfaces one focus for the day based on how you slept and what's ahead.

12:00 – 15:00

Midday

A short pulse check when energy typically dips. If stress is climbing, Cadence offers a 2-minute breathing reset.

20:00 – 22:00

Evening

A reflection prompt closes the loop — what helped, what didn't — and quietly feeds tomorrow's morning check-in.
